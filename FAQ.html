Frequently Asked Questions

Tattoo FAQ

What is your shop rate?

Our tattoo rates are $150/hr for up to 3 hours, and then $130/hr for anything over 3 hours. Minimum charge is $80.

Do I need to pay a deposit? How much do I need to pay?

Deposits are REQUIRED to book any tattoo appointment; if you do not leave a deposit, you do not have an appointment!
Deposit amounts vary on size and time required, but are generally half of the required time slot booked.
The minimum deposit is $80. Deposits can be paid in person, via credit card over the phone, or via email transfer if you live out of town.
VERY IMPORTANT: Deposits are 100% NON-REFUNDABLE! It is not the fault of the artist if you do not follow the Tattoo Policies, their time is valuable.
Please Contact us if you have further questions about deposits.

What kind of art do you do? Do you tattoo flash? Can I change my design after I have booked?

We are a custom shop, and so most of what we do is custom. We have done flash, but we try to put our own spin on it to make it truly unique.
We do have to pre-approve all artwork that is to be drawn and tattooed. If we feel that a piece is not up to our standards, we will refuse to do that work.
You cannot change your design once you have booked your tattoo.

Piercing FAQ

What are your piercing prices?

All piercing prices are located on the Piercing Prices page.

How old do I have to be to get a piercing?

You must be 13 years old WITH PARENTAL CONSENT for an ear, navel, nose, or lip piercing. The parent must have their ID, and the child must also have a piece of ID with their name on it. If last names do not match, you must present something that proves matching addresses. We do not let anyone else sign for minors including grandparents, siblings, friends, aunts, or uncles.
You must be 16 to sign for yourself for an ear, navel, nose, or lip piercing.
You must be 18 to sign for yourself for any other piercing, including intimate piercings.
We will not do cheek piercings, sorry!

How do I take care of my piercing?

You can find piercing aftercare information on the Piercing Aftercare page, or contact us and we would be happy to assist!
