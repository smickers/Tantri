Mike Thompson-Hill

Mike Thompson-Hill was born and raised in Saskatchewan, originally from Assiniboia. He started his career in Moose Jaw with Madtatter's, but moved to Saskatoon in 2002 with his wife Leanne Thompson-Hill to start Tantrix. He has been tattooing for over 17 years and loves to tattoo anything with colour, including:
  • Butterflies
  • Flowers
  • Wildlife
  • Portraits
  • Strange and unusual things
  • Horror movie portraits
  • Skulls
  • Evil images
  • Birds
  • Ocean scenes
Generally his wait time is 4 to 6 months, but can sometimes fit in smaller pieces. Contact the shop for availability!
